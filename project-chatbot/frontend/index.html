📚 Learn

What is a period?
A period is when your body sheds blood from the uterus. It usually happens once a month.

What is puberty?
Puberty is when your body starts to grow and change into an adult body.

Is it normal to feel cramps?
Yes! Mild cramps are common. Rest, warmth, and hydration can help.
